Rock Scroll Dagger

The warm light has gone and the cold November air begins covering the woods with frost as you make your way back home. You feel the darkness creep upon you and the fireflies which once flew about close to you have disappeared. You feel a sudden chill not caused by the cold but by the presence of someone ... or rather something ... close by. And then you hear it.

'Are you leaving so soon?' You realize this 'voice' is not speaking to you but rather you hear it inside your head.

'How about this' says the voice. 'If you beat me at the old tavern game of Rock Scroll Dagger, I will let you leave. However, if you do not... well, let's not spoil the surprise shall we'.

You weren't prepared for this. All your satchel holds is a peculiar looking stone, a scroll with illegible writing on it and ah... yes... a dagger! Albeit, a dagger which requires sharpening.

Feeling faint you desperately search your satchel for something useful. Your hand finds a spherelike object swishing with fluid. You realize you have a health potion!

You must decide whether to drink the potion and hope it goes unnoticed or continue fighting.

With one swift movement you pop the cork from the bottle and vigourosly consume the syrupy contents.

What do you reach for?

HEALTH
