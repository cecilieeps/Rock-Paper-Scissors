Rock Scroll Dagger

"The sun has just retreated and the cold November air begins covering the woods with frost as you make your way back home. You feel the darkness creep upon you and the fireflies which once flew about close to you have disappeared. You feel a sudden chill not caused by the cold but by the presence of someone ... or rather something ... close by. And then you hear it.

'Are you leaving so soon?' You realize this 'voice' is not being spoken to you rather you hear it inside your head.

'How about this' says the voice. 'If you beat me at the old tavern game of Rock Scroll Dagger, I will let you leave. However, if you do not...well, let's not spoil the surprise shall we'.

You weren't prepared for this. All your satchel holds is a peculiar looking stone, a scroll with illegible writing on it which you randomly found earlier and ah... yes... a dagger! Albeit, a dagger which requires sharpening."

'Feeling faint you desperately search your satchel for something useful. Your hand finds a spherelike object swishing with fluid. You realize you have a health potion!'

It is up to you to decide whether to drink the potion or to continue fighting.

You win!

Oh no ... something dark reaches from beyond the trees...

What do you reach for?

Your health
